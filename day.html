Experience the thrill & magic of Castlevania in brilliant daylight!

What is the Day Pass?

The Castlevania Day Pass gives you access to a full day of exciting rides, enchanting water park fun, and everything you need to make the most of your day under the sun.

Why is it Popular?

  • 🏖️ Enjoy unlimited water park access & swimming sessions.
  • 🎢 Thrilling rides and attractions throughout the day.
  • 🌞 A bright and vibrant daytime ambiance to enhance your experience.

Why Choose the Day Pass?

  • ✔️ Includes unlimited rides and water park activities for a complete experience.
  • ✔️ Enjoy a 15% discount on food and refreshments at the food court.
  • ✔️ Free parking, lockers, and sunshade areas.
  • ✔️ Perfect for families, couples & adventure seekers looking to make the most of their day.

Pass Details & Facilities

💰 Price: 4000 Rs per pass.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Admission: 2 Adults & 2 Kids per pass.

🏊 Swimming: Water park open with swimming lanes and slides. Swimming clothes will be provided for all visitors.

🎢 Rides: Access to all thrilling rides throughout the day, from roller coasters to water slides.

🎟️ Coupons: Get a 10% discount on your next visit!

🚗 Parking: Complimentary for Day Pass holders.

🔒 Lockers: Secure lockers available for your belongings.
